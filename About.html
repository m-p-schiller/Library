About

Welcome! Unicorn Bookstore is a digital library service that allows you to download freely download books for your enjoyment. It was created in 2019 by four university students who where doing a computer science project on the subject of development, and decided that they wanted do create a website that would fulfil this purpose while being appealling and easy to navigate.

This project is still currently being worked on, so please understand that it will be undergoing changes, and that what you see here is not a final project, but simply the most recent revision of it.
